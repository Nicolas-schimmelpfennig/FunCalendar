FunCalendar

A beautiful countdown widget for iOS

About the App

FunCalendar is an iOS widget app that displays a stylised monthly calendar on your home screen. Set a deadline and watch the days count down with a colourful progress bar — right from your lock screen or home screen.

The app is developed by Nicolas Schimmelpfennig.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my deadline?

Open the FunCalendar app, enable the "Deadline active" toggle, and pick a date using the date picker. The widget updates automatically.

The widget isn't updating. What do I do?

Try opening the app and making a small change to any setting — this forces the widget to refresh. If it still doesn't update, remove and re-add the widget from your home screen.

I purchased the Lifetime License but customisation is still locked.

Open the app and tap "Restore Purchase". This retrieves your previous purchase from the App Store at no charge.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes. The Lifetime License is tied to your Apple ID, not a single device. Sign in with the same Apple ID and use "Restore Purchase" on each device.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a refund request.
