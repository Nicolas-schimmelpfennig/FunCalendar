Overview

FunCalendar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected by design — the app has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

This policy applies to the FunCalendar iOS app and its associated WidgetKit extension, developed by Nicolas Schimmelpfennig.

Data We Do Not Collect

FunCalendar does not collect any of the following:

• Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
• Location data
• Usage analytics or crash reports
• Device identifiers
• Any data transmitted to external servers

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores your widget settings — such as your selected deadline, start date, colour preferences, and display options — locally on your device using iOS system storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

To delete this data, simply delete the app from your device.

In-App Purchases

FunCalendar offers a one-time in-app purchase (Lifetime License). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. FunCalendar does not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information.

Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party SDKs and Analytics

FunCalendar does not include any third-party analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. No third party receives any data as a result of your use of this app.

Children's Privacy

FunCalendar does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As the app collects no data from any users, it is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
